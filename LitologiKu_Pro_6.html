Referensi Standar

Parameter dan klasifikasi dalam aplikasi ini didasarkan pada standar geologi internasional berikut:

Batuan Beku

  • Streckeisen, A. (1976) — To each plutonic rock its proper name. Earth-Sci. Rev. 12: 1-33. (Klasifikasi QAPF)
  • Le Maitre, R.W. et al. (2002) — Igneous Rocks: A Classification and Glossary of Terms. Cambridge Univ. Press. (IUGS Edisi ke-2)
  • Le Bas, M.J. & Streckeisen, A. (1991) — The IUGS systematics of igneous rocks. J. Geol. Soc. London 148: 825-833.

Batuan Sedimen

  • Wentworth, C.K. (1922) — A scale of grade and class terms for clastic sediments. J. Geology 30: 377-392. (Skala ukuran butir)
  • Folk, R.L. (1959, 1962) — Practical petrographic classification of limestones. AAPG Bull. (Klasifikasi batuan karbonat)
  • Dunham, R.J. (1962) — Classification of carbonate rocks. AAPG Memoir 1: 108-121.
  • Pettijohn, F.J. et al. (1987) — Sand and Sandstone. Springer-Verlag. (Klasifikasi sandstone)
  • Schmid, R. (1981) — Descriptive nomenclature and classification of pyroclastic deposits and fragments. Geol. Rundschau.

Batuan Metamorf

  • Fettes, D. & Desmons, J. (2007) — Metamorphic Rocks: A Classification and Glossary of Terms. Cambridge Univ. Press. (IUGS SCMR)
  • Miyashiro, A. (1994) — Metamorphic Petrology. UCL Press.
  • Yardley, B.W.D. (1989) — An Introduction to Metamorphic Petrology. Longman.

Deskripsi Lapangan Umum

  • British Standard BS 5930 (2015) — Code of practice for ground investigations. (Derajat pelapukan I - VI)
  • ISRM (1981) — Rock Characterization, Testing and Monitoring: ISRM Suggested Methods.
  • Tucker, M.E. (2011) — Sedimentary Rocks in the Field. Wiley. (Panduan deskripsi lapangan)
  • Compton, R.R. (1985) — Geology in the Field. Wiley.

Referensi Gambar

  • Granulitas https://sites.pitt.edu/~cejones/GeoImages/
  • Struktur Sedimen — https://www.geologypage.com/; https://geo.libretexts.org/ ; https://geologyistheway.com/ ; https://www.researchgate.net/
  • Tekstur Batuan beku — https://geologybase.com/ ; https://www.geologyin.com/
